About Wakuthii Waste Management

Your trusted partner in keeping Embu clean and green since 2015

Our Story

Founded with a vision to transform waste management in Embu County

Wakuthii Waste Management started as a small community initiative in 2015, responding to the growing need for professional garbage collection services in Embu Town. What began with just two trucks and five dedicated employees has now grown into Embu's leading waste management company.

Today, we serve over 5,000 satisfied customers across Embu County, collecting more than 50 tons of waste daily. Our commitment to excellence, environmental sustainability, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for homes, businesses, and institutions.
